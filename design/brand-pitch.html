06 — Voice
Talk like the operator next to you.
DO Be specific.
"Recovered key 'northwind2023' validates on auth.northwind.io." Not "potential auth issue."
DO Show your work.
Every finding links to evidence. Every action links to the turn that produced it. No magic.
DON'T Manufacture drama.
No "🚨 Critical alert!" copy. No exclamation points in error states. Severity comes from the score, not the tone.
DON'T Sell.
Operators recognize marketing copy in three seconds and tune it out. Write like a colleague handing you a finding.
